Classes

Baking 101

Sign up to learn the principles. The tips and tricks you take away from this class will be valuable in making both sweet and savory baked goods. All materials and ingredients are provided. Just bring yourself and an idea of a dessert you would like to make.

Price: $125 per person, $200 for couples

The Finesse of Frosting a Cake

So you can bake. But can you decorate a cake? We are talking about utilizing the different equipment and techniques that can pass off any home-baked desserts as bakery-bought. In class we will decorate a mini cake and a half-dozen cupcakes. All ingredients will be provided. Pastry bags and tips will be available for purchase during the session. If you already have these items, bring them along to the class.

Price: $75 per person

Events

The Wedding Cake Experience

Special Guest: Sara Joseph

With over 20 years of wedding planning and cake consulting experience, Lori has for the first time shared her guidance that has benefited some of the most prominent A-List weddings. Her visit will include a discussion of dessert alternatives and the reading of an excerpt of her bestseller. Books will be available for purchase and signing.

The Gluten-Free Life

Speaker: Lorie Hillson, Ph.D.

Join us as we learn about the different facets of having a diet without gluten. Dr. Hillson will be speaking about her research into the nutritional and health benefits of this diet. The event will include a Q & A session with the professor and a mystery guest. Gluten-free refreshments will be served.
