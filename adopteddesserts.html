Why Adopt a Dessert?
Let's call this the Ebay of cakes. This is the section of our website where we allow our customers with extenuating circumstances to post their cakes and dessert orders for resale. Whether you are seeking a dessert in the 11th hour before an event, looking for a new direction, or shopping for a bargain dessert assortment, you are in the right place! We give a limited window of time for resale to preserve the freshness of these baked goods. All Adopted Dessert orders are accepted via phone and in-person ONLY.
