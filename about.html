About Tricia's
My name is Denise Richards, owner of Tricia's Bakeshoppe. The bakeshoppe is named after my mother Patricia who encouraged my love of baking from a young age. Years later, that love has manifested into a bakeshoppe with a strong customer following. Tricia's operates both as a cafe/bakery with a rotating menu of diverse desserts. We cater to people of all dessert preferences; whether you have a sweet tooth (like me!) or perfer desserts with less sugar (like my best friend and business partner Beth), there is a dessert for everyone.
Our Committment
For over the ten years the bakeshoppe has provided clientele spanning the country with cakes crafted to their respective events needs. Ingredients are sourced from vendors committed to substainability. Our team comprises individuals with both extensive palettes of flavors and through knowledge pastries and desserts of various cultures. Conclusively, this means desserts made with the freshest ingredients, of the highest-quality, and uniquely suited to each customer.
