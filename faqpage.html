Q: I want a cake but do not know where to start. What should I do?

A: The trend now is vision boards and Pinterest. We also have two quizzes to give you an idea about style and flavor profiles.


How far in advance should I schedule an appointment for a cake?

The sooner the better. We generally advise clients to make an consultation appointment 2-3 months in advance. For spring/summer events, we recommend an additional 1-2 months.


Q: My event is cancelled. Am I entitled to a refund?

A: Because we take the time to handcraft each cake and/or dessert order to an event's specifications, we do not process refund requests. However, we do have options:

  • Donate the order to a local charity.
  • Stomp on your wedding cake!
  • Resale your masterpiece as an Adopted Dessert.

Q: I have a last minute event and there are no appointments available. Help me please?

A: We would love to bail you out of this situation. We have all been in the 11th hour asking for an extension. We will do our best to accomodate you, however other factors (e.g. our cakeload, nature of the request, event date) might prohibit us.

If you are flexible, check out Adopted Desserts page, where you can give "desserted" cakes and pastries a new event! Don't worry, all sales are guaranteed fresh!


Q: Do you accomodate allergies and dietary restrictions?

A: Absolutely. Please indicate and allergy and dietary restrictions during the consultation. Also, we advise you to order as soon as possible to give us an opportunity to designate a portion of our kitchen to process your order to prevent cross-contamination.


Q: I am looking at some cakes yet I do not see anything that catches my eye.

A: Our catchphrase here is "mix and match." Browse our photo gallery of past works and jot down elements of different cakes. Like the edible pearls from one cake with the lace trim of another cake? Tell us! Also, feel free to bring ideas and pictures to the consultation appointment and we can craft an idea to suit your needs!


Q: Besides cake, what else do you have?

A: Options! We have dessert tables, macarons towers, cake pops, naked cakes, a croquembouche, ice cream sandwich bar, etc.


Q: Does the store offer cake decorating classes?

A: During the fall and winter seasons, we offer bakery and cake decorating classes. Check out our Classes & Events page for more information. First come, first served.


Q: Do you offer internships for culinary arts students?

A: Each year, we offer two (2) internships for the summer and one (1) internship for the fall. We invite individuals of all backgrounds to apply. Enrollment in an culinary arts program is not required.


Forget an internship. I need a job!

[Laughs] We appreciate the honesty! However, our staff is very loyal, so openings are few and far between. Feel free to check with us via our Contact page.


Q: Who do I contact if I have questions about an existing order.

A: Please contact us via email: info@bakeshoppe.com and include your Order # in the subject line of the email.
